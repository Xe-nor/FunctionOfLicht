Online Consultancy

You want to redesign your space, rejuvenate your lighting and thereby lifestyle with f(Licht), yeah that is one of the best discussion you have taken! And now, nothing can stop us from working together to give your dream a shape. Even if you cannot arrange a site visit for our team, there is no need to worry! Although a site visit is essential, just like a physical inspection of a doctor of his patient.  However, online consultancy is as effective and as good as telemedicine! We will stay connected with you throughout the project and after its completion. We will provide all the design ideas, and through video conferencing, we will also assess the progress of your project and suggest necessary measures whenever it is required.

Our remote lighting consultation allows you to meet with our expert designers remotely. You and your designer will discuss your needs and vision for light. You will receive a custom design with recommendations tailored to your unique property and preferences and a bid. The method can be installed at once or in phases if you need the installation and commissions under our supervision.
